Recovery

Recovery is the most important part of training. If you don't recover properly, you won't be able to train as hard as you want to. Whoop is a great tool to track your recovery and make sure you're not overtraining.

Sleep

{{whoop_sleep}}

Sleep is the most important part of recovery. Whoop tracks your sleep and gives you a sleep score. It also tells you how much sleep you need to get to be fully recovered.
